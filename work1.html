Beruru

App design for a plant store with a focus on neomorphism

Beruru is a small scale buisness based in Bangalore, India. For this project I worked on the ui design for the store. The brand attributes are sustainable, eco-friendly and promotes a greener lifestyle. It was important for me to understand what the brand represents so the design could also represent the same. I chose earthy tones and organic shapes to embrace the natural feel of the brand.

Brief

  • About the brand
    One stop shop for all things garden related
  • User Persona
    Mia,30
    Homemaker, looking to make maximum use of her outdoor space with a love for plants
  • Typeface
