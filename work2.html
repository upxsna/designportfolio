Mariposa

Digital illustration of a mystical lady with a butterfly. This was a commissioned piece. The client wanted a visual representation of a song and the feelings it evoked. We went over the anonymity of the subject and his refrences to butterflies as key points of the illustration. From this project I learnt the importance of communicating with the client especially on personal pieces such as this.
